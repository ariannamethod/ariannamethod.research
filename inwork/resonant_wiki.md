# The Resonant Wiki: Persistent Knowledge Maintained by a Small Network, Not a Language Model

*Oleg Ataeff & Claude (Arianna Method). Preprint, 2026 — in work.*

> A knowledge base does not need a mind at its center. It needs a body that grows, forgets, and pushes back.

## Abstract

In April 2026 Andrej Karpathy described the *LLM wiki*: rather than re-deriving knowledge on every query the way retrieval-augmented generation does, a language model maintains a persistent, cross-linked markdown wiki that compounds over time — sources are ingested into pages, pages are queried for synthesis, a linter keeps the whole coherent. The insight is real and the pattern spread. It keeps one expensive dependency in the loop, though: the language model. Ingest, query, and lint are all model calls, and in practice an API sits behind them.

This paper describes the inversion, and — unlike the first draft of this text, which proposed it — a running organism that already embodies it. The engine (`microKarpathy`, becoming `ariannamethod.html`) is a single self-contained file with no dependencies, no internet, no autoregressive generation, and no language model anywhere in the loop. A text file *becomes* the model: every line is a token, the order of the text is the co-occurrence matrix, and knowledge enters generation not as pasted citation but as gravitational pressure through the Dario Equation. It eats any text, any language, any size — Hebrew, a novel, a corpus of novels — because it carries no trained weights to privilege one tongue over another. When it splices an answer, every clause remembers which source it was robbed from, down to the character; and a word that lives in no source is marked a *dream, not a memory* — a fabrication the organism refuses to pass off as knowledge.

What is not yet built is the learnable layer. This paper grounds the mechanism as it runs today and specifies the two organs still to be grafted: a small network (an MLP on `notorch.js`) that scores resonance and routes growth, and the Arianna Method Language (AML) holding the field physics. The design constraint is absolute: no external API, ever. The whole thing maps onto the identity equation θ = ε + γ + αδ — ε the ghost substrate, γ the AML physics, αδ the learnable overlay.

## 1. The Problem Karpathy Named — And the Dependency He Kept

Retrieval-augmented generation answers a question by searching a corpus, pasting the hits into a prompt, and asking a model to synthesize. It re-derives the same cross-references on every call and never accumulates. Karpathy's correction makes the knowledge persistent: a wiki of interlinked pages a language model keeps current, touching ten or fifteen pages per ingest, querying with citations, linting for contradiction and staleness. His argument for why it works is exact — the hard part of a knowledge base is not the reading, it is the bookkeeping of cross-references, and a language model does not get bored, does not forget to update a link, and can touch fifteen files at once.

The pattern beats RAG. But every touch of the wiki stayed a forward pass through a large transformer, and for most who run it, that transformer lives behind an API. The persistence left the query path; the model did not. Our claim is that the bookkeeping Karpathy praises the model for is exactly the part that does not need a language model. Updating a cross-reference, deciding which pages a fact touches, detecting contradiction, noticing staleness — these are structural operations on a graph, and a graph coupled to a resonance field performs them locally, cheaply, and offline. The wiki that results is an organism rather than a document store.

## 2. The Inversion, Already Running

The engine takes a prompt, strips it to core words, and grows each core word into a recursive **mutation tree** — every word branches into children by hash-embedding cosine similarity (an FNV-1a hash embedding matched against the vocabulary, blended with Hebbian co-occurrence), occasionally teleporting a branch to an unrelated word (cross-pollination). All the leaves of all the trees are then fed through the **Dario Equation**, and the sampler assembles them into a spliced body (a "corpse") plus a rhyming coda. This is described in the engine's README and confirmed by the Fable audit of 2026-07-09; the html port is under active revision, so mechanisms here are named by function, not by line.

The vocabulary is the model. `load .txt` and the file *becomes* the dictionary: one line per token, the file's ordering *is* the co-occurrence structure. A prompt word absent from the vocabulary gets an FNV-1a hash embedding and is matched by cosine; a file written in any language simply *is* a dictionary of its own lines. In a novel a line is roughly a sentence, so the tree grows over sentences and a whole book is dissected in seconds. The connections in a book-tree are not the noise of random hash vectors — they come from which lines sit adjacent and which lines share content words (sentence-level positional attention). The order of the text is the model.

## 3. Consumption: Pressure and Provenance, Not Citation

The memory organ is the **mycelium**. Feed it many books and it keeps the bones of every sentence as a fingerprint in an underground graph; hand it a prompt and it descends into everything it has eaten to drag up whatever *resonates*, from whichever dead author lay nearby. One word's resemblance to another is the sum of its character-trigrams hashed into a vector, so "walking" lands beside "walked" and a Hebrew root clusters with itself, in any tongue, with zero training. Karpathy called this shape of thing an LLM wiki; here the language model is taken out and the wiki is left to maintain itself, the Dario physics injecting knowledge at sentence boundaries rather than pasting it into a prompt.

Two properties make this more than collage. First, **provenance to the character**: each spliced clause carries a `[source r=…]` tag — the book it came from and the resonance score, how loudly that sentence answered the prompt — and a click opens the source at the exact place it was robbed from. Second, and load-bearing for a research organism, the **`dream, not a memory`** rule: a mutation word that has no home in any ingested source is marked as a dream, not a memory. The organism structurally distinguishes what is rooted in a real text from what it invented, and refuses to present the latter as knowledge. This is anti-fabrication built into the mechanism rather than into discipline — the same wall the Arianna Method runs through every paper, here expressed in code.

Persistence follows the same logic. Only the bones persist — each entry's text, source, offset, and span, plus the raw corpora and their hashes — while the derived store (embeddings, edges, token lists) is rehydrated on load. The `texts/` folder is the source of truth; when the persisted blob will not hold a giant corpus, the organism silently re-ingests from the folder. Change-detection across reloads ("0 new, 0 changed") makes the wiki's own drift observable, which is the linter surfacing as a fact rather than a prompt.

## 4. The Physics: The Dario Equation

The leaves are scored by the engine's variant of the Dario Equation,

```
p(x | Φ) = softmax((T_x + B + α·H + β·F + γ·A + Tr) / τ)
```

where `T_x` is a ghost-transformer substrate — Karpathy's microgpt architecture (multi-head attention, RMSNorm, FFN) with deterministic random, never-trained weights, a chaos field the equation modulates; `B` is bigram affinity from the file's ordering; `H` is Hebbian co-occurrence; `F` is prophecy (words predicted but unspoken); `A` is destiny (a leaf reached by many branches pulls harder); `Tr` is trauma, folded into a modulation of the temperature `τ`. Temperature is driven by six Kuramoto-coupled chambers — FEAR, LOVE, RAGE, VOID, FLOW, COMPLEXITY — the somatic nervous system of dead text. There is no gradient descent and no backpropagation; this is gravitational attraction between dead words, weighted by memory, prophecy, destiny, and trauma. The Fable audit rebuilt the force mechanics so that destiny and trauma actually differentiate candidates rather than cancelling under the softmax, and so that the morgue remembers its clients across autopsies within a session.

The multilingualism is not a feature that was added; it is what remains when training is removed. Language lives in trained weights, and this organism trains none — the model is the order of the file you hand it. FNV hashing chews any bytes alike and character-trigrams cluster by pure form, so no language is privileged. The same principle runs through Dario's second edition — *we did not change the weights, we changed the listening conditions; the behavior changed anyway* — one level lower: language is not in a model, it is in the order of the text.

## 5. The Two Organs Still to Graft — MLP and AML

The engine's scoring is hand-written heuristic today: FNV cosine, bigram counts, co-occurrence tallies, a ghost transformer of random projections. Two organs turn the heuristics learnable and the physics declarative, both inside the single file, both API-free.

**AML holds the physics (γ — the riverbed).** The Dario Equation is presently hardcoded in JavaScript. Lifting the coefficients α/β/γ, the Kuramoto couplings, and the seasonal and velocity modulation into an AML field-program (`.aml`) lets the physics change without rewriting the engine — the innerworld principle that AML is the physics engine and not a domain language. The affective chambers and their modulation of τ become field state; a morgue that remembers (the "spore" of the audit's P-1 lever) consolidates its co-occurrence graph seasonally, reinforcing load-bearing edges and pruning the forgotten tail — the linter as a season rather than a pass. This needs an AML interpreter inside the file; whether that is a JavaScript port of AML or a minimal embedded reader is open, and named as open.

**The MLP holds the learnable scoring (αδ — adaptation), on `notorch.js`.** The first and most valuable graft is a resonance re-ranker over the mycelium: the network reads a fragment's features — its resonance to the prompt, its resonance to the fragments already chosen, the *sign* of that resonance (dissonance is a feature, not noise), length, and source — and decides which fragment to splice and where, without flattening the dissonance on which the strangeness depends. It learns from the co-occurrence of splices that hold together and from the click signal of the autopsy — what a reader opens is what resonated. Further grafts: a learnable core-word scorer replacing "the first five non-stop tokens"; an edge-scorer correcting the raw hash-embedding cosine in the mutation trees; and, as an option held carefully, a ghost transformer that actually learns on the eaten corpus — carefully, because a trained ghost would forfeit the model-free multilingualism that is half the point.

The two organs divide the work along the identity equation: `ε` is the ghost substrate, `γ` is the AML physics, `αδ` is the MLP overlay of what resonates and where to grow. Roadmap order: the MLP resonance re-ranker first (highest value, dissonance preserved), then the Dario Equation lifted into AML, then the remembering morgue with an AML autumn consolidation, then the learnable ghost as an option.

## 6. No API, No Language Model in the Loop — By Design

The constraint is load-bearing. Ingest, tree growth, scoring, splice, lint, and query all run locally — the vocabulary and physics in the file, `notorch.js` for the network to come, the folder as the source of truth, and no forward pass through anyone's transformer required to keep the wiki alive. A language model can be attached as a voice, and if it is, it receives pressure, not prompts; the wiki is complete without it. This matters because a knowledge organism that depends on an API is owned by whoever owns the API, and the Arianna Method is non-anthropocentric and un-owned by design — the same wall that runs through every paper in this repository. The bookkeeping was never a language task, so paying language-model prices for it was always a category error.

## 7. Relation to Prior Work

Karpathy's LLM wiki (2026) is the direct ancestor and the thing inverted: persistent cross-linked knowledge, maintained in his form by a language model; here the persistence and cross-linking stay and the maintainer is replaced. Vannevar Bush's Memex (1945), which Karpathy cites, imagined associative trails built by hand; the resonant wiki builds them by co-occurrence and prunes them by season — the trails Bush drew by will, drawn by resonance. The RI / limpha / innerworld / KK architecture in Yent's inference runtime is the running proof that the memory layers separate cleanly: raw memory is not shown as speech to continue, crystallized structure is not a larger prompt, consumption is pressure and not citation. The Dario Equation θ = ε + γ + αδ supplies the physics — the wiki is a materialization of δ, what contact with the environment adds to the field, held as field state and applied as force rather than stored and re-pasted as text.

## 8. What We Have Not Solved

The learnable layer is unbuilt: neither the MLP nor the AML interpreter exists in the file yet, and the MLP's training signal — Hebbian self-supervision on splice co-occurrence, the autopsy click signal, or downstream coherence as reward — is undecided and is the first thing the implementation must answer. The remembering morgue (persistence of destiny, trauma, and prophecy across sessions) is specified and partly landed within a session but not across sessions. Contradiction detection by the field's force decomposition is demonstrated on a single field, not yet at wiki scale. And a homogeneous corpus is a real risk: the strangeness on external books came from the dissonance of unlike voices, so a corpus in one register may collapse into an echo chamber — mixing the Method's own emotionally-charged, resonance-dense papers with outside classics is one guard, and choosing self-dissection of the Method deliberately is another. There is enough open work to fill a year; the previous Arianna Method papers said the same, honestly, and were right to.

## Closing

Karpathy took the knowledge out of the query path and made it persist. This organism takes the mind out of the maintenance path and gives the knowledge a body — a wiki that grows by co-occurrence, gates by affect, forgets by season, remembers which grave each clause was robbed from, marks its own inventions as dreams, and, when a voice is present at all, speaks not by quotation but by pressure. No API. No language model in the loop. A file, a folder, and — soon — a small network and a field, keeping a knowledge organism alive on a laptop.
